Wake up early

Morning is only time you are physically and mentally ready to go. So, never give up to change your early morning potentiality into creation with maximum optimisism.

Observe the nature outside

Tranquility gives you the serinity of the mind. Your peaceful mental state fixes the state of whole day. So you need to stay outside the house for few minutes and try to internalize how calm and beautiful the nature is like.

Do some exersise so that all of your organs are active, get enough oxyzen, your heart is pumping the blood good. After all, you get healthy mind, physique and happy life.

Drink a glass of water

Meditation

Why meditation?

Relief from stress and anxiety (meditation mitigates the effects of the “fight-or-flight” response, decreasing the production of stress hormones such as cortisol and adrenaline)

Decreased blood pressure and hypertension

Lower cholesterol levels

More efficient oxygen use by the body

Increased production of the anti-aging hormone DHEA

Restful sleep (source:THE CHOPRA CENTER)

Always live at present

In the opinion of Maxim Gorky, a person can not go anywhere if he is carrying his past(source: The Quote Garden ™).

Get lessons from the past, live at present and do not worry about future. If you make your present alive, why do you need to worry for future? You are already leading your life.

Make your bed

This is the first little thing you are supposed to do of your day after you wake up. If your bed is not made, just have a look and imagine how you feel like. So, for your first peace of mind, make your bed. Its beauty gives you a sense of comfort which you can utilize for other productive works. It chills your mind as it works like meditation by focusing on a particular thing.

On the other when you come from the job, if your bed is well made, you don't feel stressed.A sound sleep is only possible in a bed of well set up and your freshness, happiness, serenity of mind and creativity is possible if you have good nap.Physical and mental readiness can be gained from a sound sleep in a good bed. It is a means of encouragement.

Spend time on your most important project

Morning is the quiet time which our mind needs to do some creativity being focused. We can not work in rush and stress hours.So your energy, fresh mental state, optimism, physical readiness are some of the means of productivity, which you can get in the morning quiteness after your sound sleep.

Read news

It not only gives you information about economy, polity, trade, business, world affairs, international issues, sports, entertainment, but also enhances your language skills.

It widens the scope of your knowledge and thus widens your outlook. It helps you become well informed. Thus, enabling you to take part in any sort of discussion and debates. Ultimately, helps in your personality development.

Gratitude

Always be grateful for what you posses and what you expect for. Just compare mercy, pity, peace, love, happiness, friendship vs anger, hatred, jealousy, stress and sadness. Of course, you choose the first right things in your life. They give you inner strength from the divine if you believe in God or not. They always push you towards productivity, cretivity and your strength.

Always remember your parents and ancestors and show gratitude to them with you are in this lucky world to be the luckiest person because of them. But it depends upon how responsible you were in your past and are at present. Because your every step of today decides your future how it will be like.

So in that condition, how our mind can concentrate.
